## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Bluetooth Speaker Project**: Overview of the entire design process from initial concepts to manufacturing, budgeting, and branding.
- **Design Ventura Competition**: Focused on entrepreneurship and upcycling; designing a product for a museum gift shop.
- **Mechanical Systems**:
    - **Types of Motion**: Linear, reciprocating, rotary, oscillating.
    - **Gearing systems**: Spur, crown, bevel, worm, rack and pinion.
    - **CAMs and Followers**: Cylinder, pear, and snail cams; flat, roller, and point followers.
    - **Levers and Linkages**: Types 1, 2, and 3 levers; reverse and push-pull linkages; cranks.
    - **Pulleys and Belts**: Pulleys with flat and V-belts.

### Spring Term
- **Metals**:
    - **Types**: Ferrous (Iron, Steel, Stainless Steel), Non-ferrous (Aluminium, Copper, Tin), and Alloys (Brass, Pewter, Solder).
    - **Properties**: Density, strength, hardness, durability, stiffness, elasticity, conductivity, etc.
    - **Lifecycle**: Recycling, sustainability, and upcycling.
- **Manufacturing**:
    - **School Techniques**: Sawing, drilling, shearing, turning; welding, brazing, riveting; pressing, bending, and casting.
    - **Industrial Techniques**: CNC milling, turning, sheet metal folding, die casting.

### Summer Term
- **Polymers**:
    - **Types**: Thermo polymers (PET, HDPE, PVC, LDPE, PS, PP, ABS, Acrylic, TPE) and Thermosetting polymers (Silicone, Epoxy Resin, Polyester Resin).
    - **Properties**: Density, strength, hardness, durability, conductivity, etc.
    - **Lifecycle**: Recycling, sustainability, upcycling, and eco-materials.
- **Manufacturing & CAD**:
    - **Techniques**: Vacuum forming, heat bending, moulding, 3D printing.
    - **CAD Skills**: Using 3D printing to realise functional products.
