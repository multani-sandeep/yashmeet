# Maths GCSE Year 9 Topics

## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Algebra**: Simplifying expressions (including brackets and fractions); solving linear equations; linear inequalities; roots and powers.
- **Shape and Space**: Properties of triangles, quadrilaterals, and polygons; accurate constructions; similar triangles; loci.
- **Sets**: Understanding set notation and using Venn diagrams for problem-solving.
- **Number**: Mastery of standard form (scientific notation) with positive and negative indices.
- **Graphs**: Drawing and sketching straight-line graphs; solving simultaneous equations graphically and algebraically.

### Spring Term
- **Shape and Space**: Pythagoras’ theorem; Circle theorems.
- **Algebra**: Algebraic factorising; operations with algebraic fractions.
- **Graphs**: Distance-time and Speed-time graphs.
- **Trigonometry**: Introduction to trigonometry with the Tangent ratio.
- **Handling Data**: Frequency tables; managing discrete and continuous data.
- **Number**: Compound and inverse percentages.

### Summer Term
- **Algebra**: Rearranging formulae (change of subject); quadratic expressions (multiplying brackets, factorising); solving quadratic equations.
- **Graphs**: Plotting quadratic graphs; representing inequalities graphically; coordinates work (midpoints, perpendicular lines).
- **Trigonometry**: Completing the trigonometry picture with Sine and Cosine ratios.
- **Number**: Efficient calculator use; estimating; rounding; upper and lower bounds.
- **Probability**: Experimental and theoretical probability of single events using standard notation.

## 7. Circle Theorems (Revision)
- **Angle at Centre:** Angle at the centre is twice the angle at the circumference.
- **Semicircle:** Angle in a semicircle is always 90° (right angle).
- **Same Segment:** Angles subtended by the same arc at the circumference are equal.
- **Cyclic Quadrilateral:** Opposite angles sum to 180°.
- **Tangent-Radius:** Tangent is perpendicular (90°) to the radius at the point of contact.
- **Two Tangents:** Tangents from the same external point to the circle are equal in length.
- **Alternate Segment:** Angle between tangent and chord equals angle in the alternate segment.
