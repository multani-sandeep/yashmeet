# Theology and Philosophy GCSE Year 9 Topics

## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Personhood and Moral Philosophy**: Exploring membership in the moral community; scholars include Mary Anne Warren and Martha Nussbaum.
- **Morality**: Critical evaluation of **Ethical Egoism** vs. **Kantian Deontology**; investigating the duty to be ‘good’.
- **Social Justice**: Applying ethical concepts to inequality in wealth, gender, and transhumanism; developing a worldview on a "just society".

### Spring Term
- **Philosophy of Religion**: Analyzing the Cosmological, Teleological (Design), and Moral arguments for God's existence; examining the relationship between Science and Religion.
- **Atheism**: Historical and philosophical study of Atheism and Humanism (Feuerbach, Marx, Sartre); evaluating the **Problem of Evil**.

### Summer Term
- **Epistemology**: Radical questions on what is real and what can be known; free will vs. determinism (Rationalism vs. Empiricism).
- **Metaphysics in Text & Film**: Analyzing the nature of the mind through thought experiments:
    - Jackson’s ‘Mary the Colour Scientist’
    - Nagel’s ‘What is it like to be a bat?’
    - Chalmers’ ‘Philosophical Zombies’
