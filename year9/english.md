# English Year 9 Topics

## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Primary Text Type**: American Literature (Prose and Modern Drama).
- **Writing Focus**: PEA+ (Point, Evidence, Analysis+) focusing on analyzing character, language, and structure.
- **Objectives**: Produce at least three detailed, complex analytical pieces using the PEA+ framework.

### Spring Term
- **Primary Text Type**:
    - **1st Half**: Non-Fiction Unit (Persuasive writing, newspaper articles, arguments).
    - **2nd Half**: Full Shakespeare play (e.g., *Othello*, *Henry V*, *Hamlet*, *King Lear*, etc.).
- **Writing Focus**: Writing to explore, imagine, describe, argue, persuade, and review.
- **Objectives**: Produce at least three high-quality creative writing pieces for various tasks and audiences.

### Summer Term
- **Primary Text Type**:
    - **1st Half**: Passage analysis (craft of close reading, writing in the style of...).
    - **2nd Half**: Poetry Unit (focus on form, specific poets, or a selection of poetry).
- **Writing Focus**: PEA+ focusing on character, language, and structure.
- **Objectives**: Produce at least three detailed analytical pieces exploring the studied texts.

## English Language
### 1. Explorations in Creative Reading and Writing
- Reading: Analyzing fiction texts (structural features, language analysis)
- Writing: Descriptive and narrative writing

### 2. Writers' Viewpoints and Perspectives
- Reading: Analyzing non-fiction and literary non-fiction
- Writing: Writing to present a viewpoint (persuasive, argumentative)

## English Literature
### 3. Shakespeare
- Plot, character, and theme analysis
- Context and language (e.g., *Romeo and Juliet*, *Macbeth*)

### 4. 19th Century Novel
- Plot, character, and theme analysis
- Context (e.g., *A Christmas Carol*, *Jekyll and Hyde*)

### 5. Modern Texts
- Modern prose or drama (e.g., *An Inspector Calls*, *Lord of the Flies*)

### 6. Poetry
- Power and Conflict / Love and Relationships anthology
- Unseen poetry analysis
