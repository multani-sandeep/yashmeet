## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)
**Textbook**: Viva 3 Rojo (GCSE Foundation)

### Autumn Term
- **Themes**: Hobbies and entertainment; birthdays; life as a celebrity; tasks of working life.
- **Grammar**: 
    - **Tenses**: Present tense (regular and irregular); Near future; Preterite (past) tense.
    - **Skills**: Learning to use three tenses (past, present, future) together effectively.

### Spring Term
- **Themes**: Future jobs; food, diet, and healthy living; daily routines and keeping fit.
- **Global Issues**: Children’s rights; recycling and environmental problems; describing my home town.
- **Grammar**:
    - **Pronouns & Verbs**: Direct object pronouns; stem-changing and reflexive verbs.
    - **Key Structures**: *Se debe* (one must); *Me duele* (it hurts me); *Poder* (to be able to).
    - **Tenses**: Introduction to the Imperfect tense.

### Summer Term
- **Themes**: Social interaction (meeting and greeting); buying souvenirs; insight into life in Madrid; communication issues.
- **Grammar**: 
    - **Tenses**: Completion of the Future tense.
    - **Comparisons**: Using the Comparative form.
    - **Idioms**: Expressions with *tener*.

## Module 1 Assessment — Grammar & Vocabulary

### Spelling Changes in the Preterite (1st Person Singular)

Verbs ending in **CAR**, **GAR**, or **ZAR** change spelling in the *yo* preterite form to preserve the original pronunciation.

| Verb ending | Yo change | Example verb | Yo preterite |
|-------------|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| -CAR | c → **qu** | buscar | **busqué** |
| -CAR | c → **qu** | tocar | **toqué** |
| -CAR | c → **qu** | sacar | **saqué** |
| -GAR | g → **gu** | llegar | **llegué** |
| -GAR | g → **gu** | jugar | **jugué** |
| -GAR | g → **gu** | pagar | **pagué** |
| -ZAR | z → **c** | empezar | **empecé** |
| -ZAR | z → **c** | cruzar | **crucé** |
| -ZAR | z → **c** | almorzar | **almorcé** |

> All other preterite endings for these verbs are fully regular (the change only affects *yo*).

---

### Regular Present Tense

Three verb types: **-AR**, **-ER**, **-IR**.

| Pronoun | hablar (to speak) | comer (to eat) | vivir (to live) |
|---------|:-----------------:|:--------------:|:---------------:|
| yo | hablo | como | vivo |
| tú | hablas | comes | vives |
| él/ella/usted | habla | come | vive |
| nosotros | hablamos | comemos | vivimos |
| vosotros | habláis | coméis | vivís |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | hablan | comen | viven |

> Note: -ER and -IR endings are identical except in the *nosotros* and *vosotros* forms.

---

### Irregular Present Tense: IR, SER, HACER

These three verbs must be memorised individually.

| Pronoun | ir (to go) | ser (to be) | hacer (to do/make) |
|---------|:----------:|:-----------:|:------------------:|
| yo | voy | soy | hago |
| tú | vas | eres | haces |
| él/ella/usted | va | es | hace |
| nosotros | vamos | somos | hacemos |
| vosotros | vais | sois | hacéis |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | van | son | hacen |

> *Hacer* is only irregular in the *yo* form (hago); all other forms are regular.

---

### Radical-Changing Verbs in the Present

The stem vowel changes in all forms **except** *nosotros* and *vosotros*.

**JUGAR** (to play) — u → **ue**

| Pronoun | Conjugation |
|---------|-------------|
| yo | **juego** |
| tú | **juegas** |
| él/ella/usted | **juega** |
| nosotros | jugamos *(no change)* |
| vosotros | jugáis *(no change)* |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | **juegan** |

**PREFERIR** (to prefer) — e → **ie**

| Pronoun | Conjugation |
|---------|-------------|
| yo | **prefiero** |
| tú | **prefieres** |
| él/ella/usted | **prefiere** |
| nosotros | preferimos *(no change)* |
| vosotros | preferís *(no change)* |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | **prefieren** |
