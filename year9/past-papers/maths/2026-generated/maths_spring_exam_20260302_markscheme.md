## Question 5

**(a)** $\frac{12x^3}{4x}$

**Answer:** 3x² **[B1]**

**(b)** $\frac{x^2 - 9}{x + 3}$

**Working:**
- Factorise numerator: (x + 3)(x - 3) **[M1]**
- Cancel (x + 3): = x - 3 **[A1]**

**Answer:** x - 3

**(c)** $\frac{2}{x} + \frac{5}{x}$

**Answer:** $\frac{7}{x}$ **[B1]**

---

**(d)** $\frac{3}{x+1} + \frac{2}{x-1}$

**Working:**
- Common denominator: (x + 1)(x - 1) **[M1]**
- = $\frac{3(x-1) + 2(x+1)}{(x+1)(x-1)}$ **[M1]**
- = $\frac{3x - 3 + 2x + 2}{(x+1)(x-1)}$
- = $\frac{5x - 1}{(x+1)(x-1)}$ or $\frac{5x - 1}{x^2 - 1}$ **[A1]**

**Answer:** $\frac{5x - 1}{x^2 - 1}$ or $\frac{5x - 1}{(x+1)(x-1)}$

# SECTION D: SHAPE AND SPACE (14 marks)

## Question 11

**(a)** Pythagoras - find hypotenuse

**Working:**
- c² = 7² + 9² **[M1]**
- c² = 49 + 81 = 130
- c = √130 = 11.4 cm (1 d.p.) **[A1]**

**Answer:** 11.4 cm

---

**(b)** Ladder problem

**Working:**
- h² + 1.5² = 5² **[M1]**
- h² = 25 - 2.25 = 22.75 **[M1]**
- h = √22.75 = 4.77 m (2 d.p.) **[A1]**

**Answer:** 4.77 m

---

**(c)** Ship problem

- d² = 12² + 9² **[M1]**
- d² = 144 + 81 = 225
- d = √225 = 15 km **[A1]**

**Answer:** 15 km

## Question 12

**(a)** Circle theorem - angle at centre

**Answer:** 16°

**Reason:** The angle at the circumference is half the angle at the centre standing on the same arc **[B1 + B1]**

---

**(b)** Cyclic quadrilateral

**Answer:** 70°

**Reason:** Opposite angles in a cyclic quadrilateral sum to 180° (so 180° - 110° = 70°) **[B1 + B1]**

---

**(c)** Tangent-radius

**Working:**
- Angle OTP = 90° (tangent perpendicular to radius) **[B1]**
- Angle PTO = 90° - 35° = 55° **[M1 + A1]**

**Answer:** 55°

**Reason:** A tangent to a circle is perpendicular to the radius at the point of contact

## Question 13

**(a)** Elimination method

**Working:**
- 3x + 2y = 13
- 5x - 2y = 11
- Adding: 8x = 24 **[M1]**
- x = 3 **[A1]**
- Substitute: 3(3) + 2y = 13, 2y = 4, y = 2 **[A1]**

**Answer:** x = 3, y = 2

---

**(b)** Substitution method

**Working:**
- Substitute y = 2x + 1 into 3x + 2y = 16 **[M1]**
- 3x + 2(2x + 1) = 16
- 3x + 4x + 2 = 16
- 7x = 14
- x = 2 **[A1]**
- y = 2(2) + 1 = 5 **[A1]**

**Answer:** x = 2, y = 5
