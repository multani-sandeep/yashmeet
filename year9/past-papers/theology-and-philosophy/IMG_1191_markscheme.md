# Theology and Philosophy
## Year 9 Autumn Term – Common Assessment Task – Mark Scheme

### Attainment Grade: Exceeding (E)
* I confidently explain the key differences between ethical egoism and Kantian deontology (including both the Principle of Universalisation and the Formula of Humanity in my evaluation of the Categorical Imperative)
* I consistently apply correct technical terminology throughout my dialogue
* I make highly effective use of examples to help illustrate my points
* I identify and evaluate the main strengths and weaknesses of both ethical egoism and Kantian deontology

### Attainment Grade: Good (G)
* I show a good understanding of some of the differences between ethical egoism and Kantian deontology (including at least one of the Principle of Universalisation and the Formula of Humanity in my explanation of the Categorical Imperative)
* I apply correct technical terminology in my dialogue
* I use at least one effective example to support a point made
* I refer to strengths and weaknesses of both ethical egoism and Kantian deontology

### Attainment Grade: Working Towards (W)
* I show some understanding of the differences between ethical egoism and Kantian deontology
* I have used some technical terminology, mostly accurately, in my dialogue
* I use an example to support a point made
* I refer to strengths and weaknesses of either ethical egoism or Kantian deontology

### Attainment Grade: Concern (C)
* I briefly outline the distinction between ethical egoism and Kantian deontology
* I rarely use technical terms in my dialogue
