# Theology and Philosophy
## Year 9 Autumn Term – Common Assessment Task – 40 mins

Task: Read the quote below from the Joker in The Dark Knight movie. This is the prompt for your dialogue – do not copy out the Joker quote. You should start with a response from Batman.

> See, their morals, their “code”... it’s a bad joke, dropped at the first sign of trouble. They’re only as good as the world allows them to be. I’ll show you. When the chips are down, these, uh, these “civilized people,” they’ll eat each other. See, I’m not a monster. I’m just ahead of the curve... The only sensible way to live in this world is without rules.

**Write a dialogue between Batman and the Joker evaluating the Joker’s claim above.**

Things to consider:
* The Joker is an ethical egoist, the Batman is generally considered to be Kantian in his moral thinking
* Use and explain key technical concepts associated with each ethical theory e.g. Categorical Imperative
* Identify the most significant strengths and weaknesses of each position.
* Use examples of moral dilemmas to illustrate the arguments
* Contrast Kantian deontology with any form of Utilitarianism to highlight strengths/weaknesses
