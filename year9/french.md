# French Notes

## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Free Time**: Activity-based verbs (jouer à/de, faire + du/de la/des); adverbs of frequency; present tense revision.
- **Friendships**: Comparatives and superlatives; revision of perfect and near future tenses.
- **Future Plans**: expressing intentions using the future tense; Pour + infinitive, Vouloir + infinitive.

### Spring Term
- **Music**: using direct object pronouns and the imperfect tense to describe present vs. past musical tastes.
- **Describing the Past**: Combining present, perfect, and imperfect tenses to describe previous life experiences (e.g., former school or home).
- **Environment**: Using the conditional tense and "Il faut/on doit + infinitive" to discuss ways to improve the world and protect the environment.
- **Conditionals**: Introduction to "Si" clauses (Si + present -> future; Si + imperfect -> conditional).

### Summer Term
- **Topic Revision**: Focus on imperfect and conditional tenses; usage of "depuis + present", "en + present participle", and expressions with "avoir".
- **Film Study**: *Sarah's Key* (analysis of a film set in WW2 and present-day Paris).
- **Exams**: Assessment of listening, reading, grammar, oral fluency, and writing skills.
