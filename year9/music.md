# Music Notes

## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Musical Theatre**: Evolution of the Broadway Musical from the early 20th century to the present; group performance projects (e.g., *Hamilton*); arranging and developing musical theatre songs.
- **Film Music**: The transition of music from stage to screen; exploring compositional techniques such as **leitmotif**, **thematic transformation**, and the use of **consonance and dissonance**; understanding **diegetic and non-diegetic sound**.

### Spring Term
- **Romantic Music (1810-1900)**: Study of harmonic repertoire and orchestration; exploration of **programme music** and nationalism; analysis of major works by Beethoven, Schubert, Wagner, and Tchaikovsky.
- **Minimalism**: 20th-century movements and techniques for developing complex pieces from simple ideas (**cyclic patterns**, **repetition**, **polyrhythm**); non-Western influences (West African and Latin American music); study of works by Riley, Reich, Cage, and Oldfield.

### Summer Term
- **Caribbean Music**: Exploring cultural influences and features of **Reggae**, **Soca**, **Merengue**, **Salsa**, **Calypso**, and **Rocksteady**.
- **Brazilian Music**: The history and cultural diversity of Samba and Bossa Nova; key features including **call and response**, **syncopation**, **ostinato**, and **polyrhythm**; ensemble performances (e.g., *Oye Como Va*).
