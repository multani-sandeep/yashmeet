# Computer Science Notes

## Curriculum Overview (2025-26)

### Autumn Term
- **Python Recap**: Review of foundational Python programming concepts.
- **Computer Crime and Cyber Security**: Legal safeguards (Computer Misuse Act, Data Protection Act, GDPR, Copyright Law); phishing scams and email frauds; hacking; safe use of social media and protecting online identity.
- **AI and Machine Learning**: Origin and history of AI; rules in decision making; ethics of AI; practical projects (image detection, chatbots).

### Spring Term
- **Web Development**: Site content gathering; introduction to HTML; website implementation and evaluation.
- **Networks**: Basic principles and architecture; LAN vs. WAN; IP addresses; Internet vs. World Wide Web; network topologies; client-server and peer-to-peer models; cloud computing; encryption techniques.

### Summer Term
- **Python Programming (Advanced)**: Modular programming using functions (with/without parameters); for loops vs. while loops; arrays (lists); debugging syntax errors; end of year exam and practical challenges.
