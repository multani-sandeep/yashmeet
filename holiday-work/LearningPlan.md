## Phase Plan

### Phase 1 — Australia, Sandeep off (8–17 Jul)
**Theme:** Set up systems and identify gaps  
**Target:** 1.5 hrs/day (lighter — settling in)

- **English:** Analyse one GCSE-style extract using PEA+ framework; identify weakest question type from Y9 English paper
- **Maths:** Revise Y9 quadratics and trigonometry
- **Sciences:** One session each (Chemistry: bonding recap; Physics: electricity recap; Biology: cells and enzymes recap)
- **History:** Re-read Y9 source analysis notes; practise inference and utility on one past paper question
- Discuss with Sandeep: which topics felt weakest in Y9 exams? Use that to adjust Phase 2 focus.

### Phase 2 — Australia, Sandeep working evenings (18 Jul – 2 Aug)
**Theme:** Build exam technique and start Y10 previews  
**Target:** 2 hrs/day

- Week of 18 Jul:
  - English: Language Paper 1 technique (creative reading + writing)
  - Maths: Quadratics, circle theorems, simultaneous equations
  - Chemistry: Moles and quantitative chemistry consolidation
  - Physics: Forces and motion Y10 preview
  - History: Extended writing structure (12-mark and 20-mark answers)

- Week of 25 Jul:
  - English: Language Paper 2 technique (non-fiction + viewpoints writing)
  - Maths: Trigonometry (all three ratios) + graphs
  - Biology: Respiration and excretion Y10 preview
  - Chemistry: Rates of reaction Y10 preview
  - History: Source utility (past paper practice)

### Phase 3 — India (4–24 Aug)
**Theme:** Past paper practice + final Y10 previews  
**Target:** 2 hrs/day (weekdays) · 3 hrs on Fri/Mon off days

- Week of 4 Aug:
  - English: Full timed Paper 1 practice (under exam conditions)
  - Maths: Probability and statistics
  - Physics: Thermal physics and magnetism Y10 preview
  - Biology: Genetics and evolution Y10 preview
  - History: Full timed question practice

- Week of 11 Aug:
  - English: Literature analysis (begin Y10 set texts if known)
  - Maths: Vectors and further algebra
  - Chemistry: Organic chemistry Y10 preview
  - Biology: Ecology and homeostasis
  - History: Full essay timed practice

- Week of 18 Aug:
  - Full review across all primary subjects
  - One past paper per subject (or timed question sets)
  - Identify any remaining gaps to flag to teachers in September
